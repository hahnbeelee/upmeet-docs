## Overview
In this guide we will be using your UpMeet link to install your meeting in ClickUp.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="List View">
    ### Step One
    Copy the link below and replace ```_YOUR_ID_``` with your Room ID that was sent to your when you signed up. 
    ```html
    https://upmeet.me/meet/clickup/?id=_YOUR_ID_
    ```

    ### Step Two
    1. In your ClickUp List press the "+ View" button and select the "Embed" option.
    2. With the link you copied with your Room ID in the previous step, paste it into the "Embed URL" field.
    3. Press the "Add View" button and join your meeting

    An example of the format is shown below:
    <Frame>
        <img src="/images/clickup-listview.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Dashboard View">
      ### Step One
      Copy the link below and replace ```_YOUR_ID_``` with your Room ID that was sent to your when you signed up. 
      ```html
      https://upmeet.me/meet/clickup/?id=_YOUR_ID_
      ```

      ### Step Two
      1. In your ClickUp Dashboard press the "+ Add Widget" button and search for the "Custom Embed" option.
      2. With the link you copied with your Room ID in the previous step, paste it into the "Embed URL" field.
      3. Press the "Add Widget" button
      4. Optional - Resize the widget to a larger size
      5. Join your meeting!

      An example of the format is shown below:
      <Frame>
          <img src="/images/clickup-dashboardview.png" />
      </Frame>

  </Tab>
</Tabs>
