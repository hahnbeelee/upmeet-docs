The Meeting UI is the simple and main part of UpMeet!

Depending on your plan it is possible to:

* Toggle your mic and cam

* Record a call (cloud or local)

* Chat with other participants

* Share reactions with all people on the call

* Share your screen in one click

* See your network stats

* Start a whiteboard


You can also press the cog in the left-hand upper corner to change settings like

* Turn on background effects

* Change your performance mode

* Change the language the UI is displayed in
